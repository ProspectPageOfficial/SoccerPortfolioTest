About Me

Vincent is a modern shot-stopper who commands his box and starts attacks with his feet. Born in Rosario, he started out as an outfield player before a growth spurt at fourteen sent him between the sticks — and he never looked back. He joined the Riverside FC academy at sixteen, made his senior debut at twenty, and has been the first-choice keeper for the past two seasons.

Off the pitch, Vincent runs a Saturday-morning goalkeeping clinic for U12s, studies sports psychology, and has a slightly unhinged collection of vintage goalkeeper gloves dating back to the early 2000s.

Strengths

  • Reflex shot-stopping and reactive saves inside six yards
  • Commanding the box on crosses and set pieces
  • 1v1 situations — patient, stays big, hands low
  • Distribution with both feet, especially long switches to the wings

Working On

  • Penalty research — building a personal database of opposition takers
